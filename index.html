What is exactly a dream house?

A dream home should be a place where the person finds comfort, no matter where they go, they will find peace at that one place. A dream house is a place that a person dreams and each day wishes to be in that place.

Steps to build your dream house:

Location


Location of the house

A good location for building a house prioritizes accessibility, safety, and an environment conducive to daily life. Consider factors like proximity to work, essential amenities, and recreational facilities. Also, factor in the type of terrain, soil stability, and climate suitability.

The land should be stable, with good drainage and not susceptible to soil erosion. It is also advisable to choose a location with different types of soil, as it will impact the quality of the house. Investigate the availability of green spaces, air quality, and the general health of the surroundings.

Layout


A Layout of a house

A house layout refers to the arrangement and design of rooms, spaces, and furniture within a home, often represented in a floor plan. It's about the overall structure and flow of the interior, determining how different areas connect and how they function.

The layout of your home can play a significant role in what it looks like and how comfortable the interiors are to live in.

Exterior Design


Exterior Design of the house

Exterior design is the process of designing the external appearance of offices, homes, commercial complexes, country houses, restaurants, stores, and other objects. This includes elements such as facades, cladding, roofs, and foundation elements.

The exterior parts of a house include doors, windows, roofing, siding, and landscaping. These visible outer components protect the interior and define the home's appearance. These elements serve functional roles, such as insulation and protection, and also contribute to the home's enhanced curb appeal.

Interior Design


Interior Design of the house

Interior design is the art and science of enhancing the interior of a building to achieve a healthier and more aesthetically pleasing environment for the people using the space. With a keen eye for detail and a creative flair, an interior designer is someone who plans, researches, coordinates, and manages such enhancement projects. Interior design is a multifaceted profession that includes conceptual development, space planning, site inspections, programming, research, communicating with the stakeholders of a project, construction management, and execution of the design.

Any project can be clearly defined by having solid principles in place. This is particularly true regarding the seven principles of interior design: balance, unity, rhythm, emphasis, contrast, scale and proportion, and details.

House Decoration

To decorate your home, start by defining your style and creating a plan that reflects your personality. Consider your color palette, select essential pieces, add artwork, and incorporate your personal touch. Don't forget to layer lighting, use mirrors strategically, and add houseplants.
